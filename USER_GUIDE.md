# NgaoMaternal Care - Quick Start Guide

## 🎯 What is NgaoMaternal Care?

A comprehensive maternal health monitoring and emergency response platform that bridges the communication gap between pregnant women and healthcare facilities through:
- 24/7 health monitoring
- Emergency alerts with GPS tracking
- Prenatal education
- AI-powered pregnancy assistant
- USSD/SMS support for areas without internet

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. Emergency Panic Button
**Location**: Mother Dashboard, Family Dashboard, USSD Option 0

**How it Works**:
1. Click/dial panic button
2. GPS location captured automatically
3. Alert sent to nearest clinic
4. Emergency contacts notified
5. Healthcare providers receive immediate notification

**When to Use**:
- Severe bleeding
- Severe abdominal pain
- No fetal movement
- Sudden collapse
- Any life-threatening emergency

### 2. Daily Health Check-ins
**Location**: Mother Dashboard → "Complete Check-in"

**What to Track**:
- Blood pressure
- Heart rate
- Fetal movement count
- Symptoms (headache, swelling, pain, etc.)
- Notes about how you're feeling

**Risk Assessment**:
- **Low**: Normal, no concerns
- **Medium**: Minor symptoms, monitor closely
- **High**: Concerning symptoms, contact provider
- **Critical**: Immediate medical attention needed

### 3. Educational Resources
**Location**: Education Page

**Content Categories**:
- General pregnancy information
- Safety and warning signs
- Nutrition and diet
- Labor preparation
- Fetal development
- Prenatal care schedule

**AI Content Suggestions**:
- Click "AI Content Suggestions" button
- Get curated articles, videos, and expert publications
- Evidence-based resources from WHO, CDC, medical journals
- YouTube educational videos
- Doctor publications

### 4. AI Pregnancy Assistant
**Location**: Floating chat button (bottom-right corner)

**What You Can Ask**:
- Pregnancy symptoms and concerns
- Nutrition and diet questions
- Exercise and lifestyle advice
- Fetal development questions
- Warning signs clarification
- General pregnancy information

**Example Questions**:
- "What foods should I avoid during pregnancy?"
- "Is it normal to feel tired in the first trimester?"
- "How can I track fetal movement?"
- "What are signs of preeclampsia?"
- "How much water should I drink daily?"

**Important**: AI provides information only, not medical diagnosis. Always consult your healthcare provider for medical concerns.

### 5. USSD Service (No Internet Required)
**How to Access**: Dial your assigned USSD code (e.g., `*384*1234#`)

**Main Menu**:
- **0**: EMERGENCY PANIC BUTTON
- **1**: I'm feeling well (daily check-in)
- **2**: I have concerns (symptom reporting)
- **3**: Medical advice (find clinic, hotline)
- **4**: View last check-in
- **5**: Educational resources

**Emergency Hotline**: 0800-MATERNAL (24/7)

## 📱 How to Use the Platform

### First Time Setup

1. **Register**:
   - Go to registration page
   - Choose your role (Mother, Family Member, Healthcare Provider)
   - Provide required information
   - Verify your account

2. **Complete Profile**:
   - Add personal details
   - Set emergency contacts
   - Add medical information (for mothers)
   - Set expected due date (for mothers)

3. **Explore Dashboard**:
   - Familiarize yourself with features
   - Read educational content
   - Try the AI chatbot
   - Locate emergency button

### Daily Routine (For Mothers)

**Morning**:
1. Complete daily health check-in
2. Track fetal movement
3. Record any symptoms

**Throughout Day**:
- Use AI chatbot for questions
- Read educational content
- Monitor for warning signs

**Evening**:
- Review check-in summary
- Plan next day's activities
- Rest and stay hydrated

### Emergency Situations

**If You Experience**:
- Vaginal bleeding
- Severe headache + blurred vision
- Severe abdominal pain
- No fetal movement
- Sudden swelling
- Difficulty breathing

**Immediate Actions**:
1. **Press Emergency Panic Button**
2. Call emergency services
3. Contact your healthcare provider
4. Alert family members
5. Go to nearest clinic/hospital

**Do NOT Wait** - Maternal emergencies require immediate attention

## 🏥 For Healthcare Providers

### Daily Workflow

**Morning Review**:
1. Check emergency alerts
2. Review flagged check-ins
3. Identify high-risk patients
4. Prioritize urgent cases

**Throughout Day**:
- Respond to new alerts
- Update patient records
- Acknowledge emergencies
- Provide guidance

**End of Day**:
- Review all flagged cases
- Update patient notes
- Plan follow-ups

### Triage System

**Priority Levels**:
- **Critical**: Immediate response (bleeding, no fetal movement)
- **High**: Same-day attention (severe symptoms)
- **Medium**: Monitor closely (multiple mild symptoms)
- **Low**: Routine follow-up (normal check-ins)
